About Us:


Dunn Rite Tree Experts LLC., specializes in tree care for residential and commercial properties in Baltimore, Carroll and Howard counties in Maryland. We have over 2 decades of tree service and landscaping experience including tree removal, pruning, cable bracing, root pruning and stump grinding. Our services are carefully planned with safety in mind and we are qualified to remove any tree regardless of size, location or condition. We own and maintain our own fleet of tree removal equipment and are equipped to service all of your needs. Dunn Rite Tree Experts LLC. are also your specialists in landscaping, irrigation and paver or concrete patios and driveways. We will work with you to plan your project with your needs and budget in mind. We have an excellent track record in customer satisfaction and we never compromise on quality and workmanship.
